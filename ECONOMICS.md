# Unit Economics & Valuation Modeling - SpendOptic

## 1. Unit Economics
SpendOptic operates a premium free evaluation funnel that monetizes through high-value enterprise AI procurement consulting and bulk consolidation services (under the **Credex** brand).

### Value Per Converted Lead (LTV)
- When a startup has potential savings > $500/month ($6,000/yr) and books a Credex procurement consultation, we negotiate bulk consolidations and multi-year contract downsizes directly with AI providers (Cursor, OpenAI, etc.).
- **Monetization Model**: We charge a performance-based success fee of **25% on first-year captured savings**.
- For a typical client saving $8,000/yr, our success fee is **$2,000**.
- **Average Lead Value (LTV)**: $2,000 per closed consultation contract.

### Customer Acquisition Costs (CAC)
- **Direct LinkedIn Outreach**: $0 (organic engineering/CFO networking).
- **SEO & Community Distribution**: $0 (organic posts, case studies, open-source content).
- **Average CAC**: $0 (completely organic, supported by partner refer networks).

### Conversion Funnel Math
- Let's assume **1,000 high-intent unique visitors** land on SpendOptic:
  1. **Form Audit Completion Rate (FACR)**: 40% (400 completed audits).
  2. **Savings threshold**: 15% of audits discover >$500/mo savings (60 high-value audits).
  3. **Lead Capture Conversion (LRSR)**: 20% of high-value audits convert to contact details / email capture (12 leads captured).
  4. **Consultation booking rate**: 33% of captured high-value leads book a Credex audit consultation (4 consultations booked).
  5. **Contract Close Rate**: 50% of consultations close (2 enterprise contracts signed).
- **Total Revenue**: 2 contracts * $2,000 average LTV = **$4,000**.
- **Revenue per Unique Landing**: $4,000 / 1,000 visitors = **$4.00 per visitor**.
- Since CAC is $0 (organic), this funnel is highly profitable on baseline unit metrics.

## 2. Path to $1,000,000 ARR in 18 Months
To reach **$1,000,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)** in 18 months, let's construct the precise arithmetic model.

### Key Assumptions
- **Contract Type**: Moving from a one-off performance fee to an **AI Procurement as a Service (SaaS)** annual subscription.
- **Subscription Price**: **$125/month ($1,500/year)** per startup customer.
- Under this subscription, we provide continuous automated seat re-auditing, subscription credential rotation, prompt caching alerts, and negotiation support.
- **Average Customer ARR**: $1,500.

### Mathematical Requirements for $1M ARR
- **Active Customers Needed**: 
  $$\text{Customers} = \frac{\$1,000,000}{\$1,500} \approx 667 \text{ active startup clients}$$

### Monthly Acquisition Timeline (18-Month Runway)
- To acquire 667 paying customers inside 18 months, we must average **37 net-new paying subscribers per month**.

### Conversion Pipeline Math Needed to hit 37 customers/month
- With a **0.8% overall conversion rate** from visitor landing to a paid $125/mo subscription (standard B2B SaaS metric):
  $$\text{Required Monthly Traffic} = \frac{37 \text{ subscribers}}{0.008} \approx 4,625 \text{ unique monthly visitors}$$

- 4,625 visitors per month equates to about **154 visitors per day**.
- Getting 154 visitors a day is highly achievable through organic community management, Fractional CFO referral networks, viral Open Graph shared Twitter updates, and targeted SEO.
- **Arithmetic Summary**: 154 daily visitors → 4,625 monthly visitors → 37 new subscribers/month (\$1,500 ARR each) → 667 active accounts inside 18 months = **$1,000,500 ARR**.
