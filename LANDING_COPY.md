## 5 Real FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

### Q1: Is my data safe? Do I need to connect our actual corporate billing portals?
**A**: Yes, your data is completely secure. SpendOptic requires **no logins, OAuth permissions, or billing portal APIs** to function. You manually enter your seating counts and selected plans. All computations are performed immediately, and any identifying company info is fully stripped on public share URLs to guarantee complete local privacy.

### Q2: How does the audit engine calculate savings recommendations?
**A**: Our engine is 100% deterministic, running specialized math models based on catalog rates verified in `PRICING_DATA.md`. We flag known overlap variables (such as running Cursor autocomplete alongside GitHub Copilot) and suggest plan tier modifications (such as Claude Max accounts downscaling to Claude Team pools).

### Q3: When is the pricing data updated? Is it accurate?
**A**: Yes. All prices are tracked directly to standard subscription listings from Cursor, GitHub, OpenAI, and Anthropic, regularly updated as of May 2026. You can review the trace table inside `PRICING_DATA.md` at any time.

### Q4: Why is there an email capture at the end? Do I have to pay?
**A**: No! SpendOptic is 100% free. We shown the full completed audit and savings figures beforehand with zero gates. The email capture is strictly optional, enabling you to lock in the audit, obtain a shareable team url, and receive notifications only when new product discounts apply.

### Q5: What is the Credex consultation CTA for audits over $500?
**A**: If our audit engine detects more than $500/mo ($6,000/yr) in wasteful runaways, your company qualifies for direct enterprise procurement negotiations with Credex specialists. They will help you unlock enterprise bulk licensing contracts, consolidate seats, and negotiate bulk API credits.
